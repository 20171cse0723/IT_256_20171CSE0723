Bazaar is an Indian Kannada action crime film directed by Suni and produced by Thimmegowda under Bharathi Film Productions banner.[1] The film stars Dhanveer Gowda,[2] a debutant and Aditi Prabhudeva in the lead roles along with Sadhu Kokila, Sharath Lohitashwa and Aruna Balaraj in the supporting roles.[3] The film's plot deals with the gambling process involved in the pigeon racing.[4] The film is shot in the real locations across Bengaluru, Mysore and Tumkur where the pigeon races are actually held.[5]

The technical crew members for the film include Ravi Basrur as the music composer, Santhosh Rai Pathaje as the cinematographer, Abhishek. M as the editor. Initially planned to release on the Sankranthi festival day, the makers postponed the release to 1 February 2019.[6]
